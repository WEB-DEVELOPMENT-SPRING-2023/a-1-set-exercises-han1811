Filipino Sopas

  • Prep: 10 min Cook: 50 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken Breasts boneless 12 oz
Cabbage sliced 1/2 piece
stalk celery Chopped 3
Onion 1 diced
Tender juicey Hotdog 3 piece sliced
Carrots 3/4 cusp diced
Evaporated milk 12 oz.
Elbow Macaroni 1 1/2 cups
Garlic 2 teaspoons minced
knorr Chicken Cube 1 piece
Water 6-8 Cups
Cooking Oil 3 Tablespoons
Salt and Ground Black Paper to taste

Step 1: Boil 3 cups water in a medium pot. Add the chicken breasts.

Step 2: Cover and cook in medium heat for 15 to 18 minutes. You may add more water if needed.

Step 3: Separate the chicken from the water. Let the chicken cool down and then shred. Set both shredded chicken and water aside.

Step 4: Heat oil in a large soup pot. Sauté the garlic until it turns medium brown. Add onion, celery, and carrot. Continue to sauté for 2 to 3 minutes.

Step 5: Add the shredded chicken. Stir and cook for 1 minute.

Step 6: Add the hot dog. Quickly stir and then pour the water used to boil the chicken. Also pour the remaining water. Let boil.

Step 7: Add the Knorr Chicken Cube. Stir. Cover and cook for 3 to 5 minutes.

Step 8: Put the elbow macaroni in the pot. Cook for 10 minutes.

Step 9: Add the cabbage. Stir and cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Note: you can add more water if needed.

Step 10: Pour the evaporated milk. Stir, let boil, cover and cook for another 3 minutes.

Step 11: Add salt and ground black pepper to taste.

Step 12: Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve.
